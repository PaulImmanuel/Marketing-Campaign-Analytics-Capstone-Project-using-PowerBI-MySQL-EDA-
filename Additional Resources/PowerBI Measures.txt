PowerBI Measures

New Measure

1. Total Customers
Total Customers = COUNT(marketing_cleaned[ID])

2. cleaned Responders
Total Responders = SUM(marketing_cleaned[Response])

3. cleaned Response Rate
Response Rate (%) = 
DIVIDE([Total Responders], [Total Customers], 0) * 100

4. Total Revenue
Total Revenue = SUM(marketing_cleaned[Total_Spend])

5. Avg Revenue per Customer
Avg Revenue per Customer = DIVIDE([Total Revenue], [Total Customers], 0)

6. Web Conversion Rate
Web Conversion Rate (%) =
DIVIDE(
    SUM(marketing_cleaned[NumWebPurchases]),
    SUM(marketing_cleaned[NumWebVisitsMonth]),
    0
) * 100
